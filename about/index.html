 · About

Collective twitter for bioinformaticians and computational biologists from their peers, new author each week

Collective twitter for bioinformaticians and computational biologists from their peers, new author each week.

We are similar to the Reddit's "ask-me-anything", but in Twitter with new opportunities. Read and discuss important and controversial questions with new authors every week.

Why?

  • Wide perspective: each author is unique, thus you will read different perspectives all the time
  • Novelty: discover new trends and inspiring people
  • 'Shuffle': just like with the Shuffle mode, dare to discover something you would have never found yourself
  • Experience: authors will share their experience and expertise
  • Paradigm shift: authors might share thoughts and tricks you have never even considered
  • Relaxed atmosphere: easy to chat with the speaker in two-way communication
  • Fun!

Subscribe to @compbiounderhood in Twitter.
